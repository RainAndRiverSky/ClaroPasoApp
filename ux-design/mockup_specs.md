"Rules of the Road" for the Designer:

# Visual Cues & Help Logic

## 1. The "Inactivity" Trigger
If no touch is detected for 5 seconds:
- The "Ear" icon pulses brighter.
- A yellow bubble appears over the primary action button.
- Audio plays: "¿Necesitas que te ayude con este paso?"

## 2. The "No-Text" Navigation
- Every button MUST have a universal icon (Camera, House, Calendar).
- Colors must follow the `color_palette.css` (Green = Go, Yellow = Help).

## 3. The "Ear" Icon Specs
- Size: At least 60dp x 60dp (Easy to hit with a thumb).
- Location: Top Right (Standard "Help" position).
- Function: Tapping the ear REPEATS the last audio summary.
