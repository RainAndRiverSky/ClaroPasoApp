# The Visual Mockup (ClaroPaso UI)

Here is the "Visual Blueprint" for the GitHub/Designer to make the app accessible for someone who cannot read:

# The "ClaroPaso" Home Screen:

 - Background: Soft, warm colors (Cream or Light Teal). No stark white (too "hospital-like").

 - The "Pulsing" Camera: A large, circular button in the center-bottom. It shouldn't just sit there—it should have a soft "glow" or pulse to draw the eye.

 - The "Ear" Icon: Top right corner. If tapped, it plays: "Toca el botón grande abajo para empezar."

 - The "Arrows": On the first launch, a bright yellow hand-drawn style arrow points to the Camera. A bubble above it says: "Sácale foto a tus papeles aquí."

 - No Menus: Avoid "hamburger" menus (the three lines) for the first-time user. Use big, friendly tiles for "Escuela," "Médico," "Legal", "Visión", "Dental" and "Citas."
