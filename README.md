# 📖 Project: ClaroPaso
"En el papeleo no solo hay datos, hay destinos. Un formulario mal llenado es una cita perdida; una cita perdida es un sueño pausado."

## 🎯 The Mission Statement
As an HSPRS (Home Study Post Release Service) Caseworker, I have seen the "American Dream" get stalled by a single sheet of paper.

Traditional apps are built for people with high digital literacy, stable internet, and "Textbook Spanish." They ignore the parent who is working two jobs, the caregiver who never had the chance to learn to read or write, and the family living in the "chaos of the transition."

## This app was designed by a Caseworker because:

I know the "Panic of the Letter": I’ve seen the fear when a court notice arrives.

I know the Literacy Gap: I understand that "Plain Spanish" isn't enough; we need Audio-First navigation.

I know the Stakes: In my world, "User Retention" isn't a metric—it's a child staying in school and a family staying together.

# 🚀 The Pitch: Dignity Through Design

## ClaroPaso is a Spanish-first, voice-guided administrative companion. It turns complex U.S. bureaucracy into a simple conversation.

## Why It’s Different (2026 Edge):
Zero-Literacy Navigation: Using pulsating visual cues (Arrows/Bubbles) and background audio guidance, the app "talks" the user through every screen.

## The "Foto-Resumen" (AI Decoder): Users take a photo of any English document. The AI interprets it instantly into warm, spoken Spanish: "No te asustes, es solo un aviso de la escuela..."

Cultural Nuance: No "robot speak." The AI sounds like a trusted comadre, offering encouragement and validating the user’s stress.

## Offline-First: Built for families with inconsistent data plans, ensuring documents are always accessible.

# 🛠 Features for the 2026 Reality
Voice-to-Task: "Recuérdame que tengo corte el martes" is all it takes to set a smart reminder.

Shared Family Hub: Allows children (the "translators") and caseworkers to securely assist with document management.

Success Audio Scripts: A behavioral design that rewards every small win with verbal encouragement to reduce "Bureaucracy Fatigue."

# 📂 Repository Structure

/prompts: The System Instructions for Google AI Studio (The "Brain").

/ux-design: Logic for the visual bubbles and onboarding arrows.

/audio-assets: Scripts for the "Human Voice" and success triggers.

/flows: Step-by-step guides for School, Health, and Legal tasks.

# 💡Pro-Tip: How to use

## To test this in Google AI Studio, upload the system_instructions.md to the System Prompt area and set the Safety Settings to 'Medium' to ensure the AI remains helpful without being overly restrictive on immigration-related vocabulary.
